Grade Levels

Elementary School (Grades K-5)

Our elementary program focuses on building strong foundations in literacy, numeracy, and social skills. We emphasize hands-on learning and exploration.

Curriculum Highlights:

  • Integrated Language Arts program
  • Singapore Math approach
  • Inquiry-based Science
  • Social Studies with global perspective
  • Arts, Music, and Physical Education

Special Programs:

  • Reading Buddies program
  • STEM Fridays
  • Outdoor Education

Middle School (Grades 6-8)

Our middle school program helps students transition to more independent learning while providing strong academic and social support.

Curriculum Highlights:

  • Advanced English and Literature
  • Algebra and Geometry
  • Life and Physical Sciences
  • World History and Geography
  • Foreign Language options

Special Programs:

  • Advisory program for social-emotional learning
  • Elective courses in arts and technology
  • Service learning projects
